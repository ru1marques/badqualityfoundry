Maria RM

Maria RM is a typeface born from the shapes engraved in the five letters of the iconic Maria biscuit, later expanded into a full alphabet with an experimental and exploratory approach while maintaining its traditional traits.The standout feature of Maria is its variable-width serifs, which create an almost choreographed movement between characters, making it highly expressive and full of personality.

Author Rui Marques
